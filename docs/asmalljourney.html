Overview

Gameplay

A Small Journey's gameplay is reminiscence of the inventory management systems of Resident Evil 4, Boulders Gates, Neverwinter Nights, etc. This grid based puzzler mixes bag management with a interesting variety of mechanics you might not expect. Put your speed, precision and organizational skills to the test.
